About Me

My name is Toghrul. I am from Azerbajjan. When I was a child, I used to watch TV shows and movies a lot. These 2 things were my passion. I watched not only what was happening on TV or in the movies, but also what was happening around me with admiration, surprise, and interest. Then I started filming what I saw with my small camera.

After that, I studied TV directing and got a BA degree to become a professional. I worked as a director, editor, producer, video editor in the TV field for more than 6 years.

A few years ago, after moving to the United States, I started my own YouTube channel, where I continued to tell stories. I made some videos about various media theories, social media, traditional media, technology and more.

Moving from a country where technology is relatively underdeveloped (Azerbaijan) to a country where technology is developing very fast (USA) increased my interest in this field even more. As a child, I watched what was happening with interest, excitement, and surprise, so in the United States, I began to watch what was happening in the technology and computer field with the same childish enthusiasm.

AI technology, new technological tools that will deeply affect human life, endless discussions about what these tools will bring to humanity, etc. increased my interest and I decided to study in this field. I am currently studying Associate degree in Software development. My goal is not only to remain a follower of what is happening in this field, but also to succeed as a professional.
